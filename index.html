About Me

I am Ben Smith - a composer, producer and BA Music and Sound Recording student at the University of York. I also perform as a snare drummer in the CLCGB National Band.
I have experience managing PA systems at sporting events and live music events, recording, mixing and producing pieces of music, as well as writing music myself.

This site allows you to explore some of my work, including my own compositions, as well as recordings, mixing and mastering that I have done - have a look at the Music Portfolio section below.
